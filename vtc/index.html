At Vauxhall Trade Club, this year we are proud to be celebrating our 25th birthday.
Established in 1991, we are the original Trade Club and for 25 years, we have delivered our customers genuine Vauxhall parts all at competitive trade prices.
To celebrate our 25th birthday and to thank you for your custom over the years, we have added 800 new parts to our core Trade Club basket of fast moving parts.
